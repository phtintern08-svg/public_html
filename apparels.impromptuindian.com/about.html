Our Journey

ImpromptuIndian began with a simple question: Why is ordering custom t-shirts so complicated?

From vague pricing and undefined delivery dates to limited fabric choices, the industry was fragmented. We decided to change that by bridging the gap between customers, local manufacturers, and delivery partners.

Today, ImpromptuIndian is a thriving platform enabling thousands of students, businesses, and creators to bring their designs to life with transparency and speed.
