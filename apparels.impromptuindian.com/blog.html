Fabric Guide

Material Matters: A ImpromptuIndian Guide

ImpromptuIndian Team
3 min read

Fabric plays a big role in the final experience of any custom apparel. It's not just about how it looks, but how it feels and performs. Cotton remains the crowd favourite for everyday comfort — soft, breathable and perfect for casual wear.

Polyester, on the other hand, works beautifully for sports, jerseys and performance-based outfits. Its durability and quick-drying capabilities make it the champion of activewear.

For those seeking the best of both worlds, Poly-cotton blends balance both material strengths — slightly structured, long lasting and excellent for printing.

Technology

Bringing Designs to Life

ImpromptuIndian Team
3 min read

Printing gives life to a design. It transforms a digital concept into a tangible reality. Some creators love vibrant DTF (Direct to Film) prints that work on almost any fabric, providing high detail and color accuracy.

Bulk teams often prefer screen printing for its clean finish and long-lasting results. Startups often choose embroidery to achieve an elegant branded look that stands out without shouting.

Trends 2025

The 2025 Style Forecast

ImpromptuIndian Team
2 min read

Fashion doesn’t remain still; it evolves. The 2025 trends show a love for minimal aesthetics — small chest prints, subtle logos, sleeve tags and tone-on-tone branding.

Streetwear influence continues to dominate, bringing oversized silhouettes, bomber jackets and neutral earthy tones to the spotlight. At the same time, winter collections are leaning toward premium hoodies and jackets with structured fits.
